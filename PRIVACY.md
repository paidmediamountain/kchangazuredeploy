# Privacy Policy — KChang Azure Deploy

**Last updated: 2026-03-19**

## What Data We Collect

**None.** KChang Azure Deploy does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

## How the Plugin Works

All operations are performed **locally on your machine**:

- The plugin calls the **Azure CLI** (`az`) installed on your computer
- Your Azure credentials are managed entirely by the Azure CLI — the plugin never reads or stores them
- Project settings (subscription ID, resource group, function app name) are stored locally in your project's `.idea/azureDeploySettings.xml` file
- No data is sent to any server operated by Ken Chang or this plugin

## Third-Party Services

- **Microsoft Azure** — the plugin communicates with Azure on your behalf via the Azure CLI. Microsoft's privacy policy applies: https://privacy.microsoft.com
- **JetBrains** — license management is handled by JetBrains. Their privacy policy applies: https://www.jetbrains.com/legal/docs/privacy/privacy/
